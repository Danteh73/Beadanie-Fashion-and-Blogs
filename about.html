Fashion Designer and Blogger

MEET THE CEO. Hi, my name is Daniel Kariuki most people call me Aj I’m a graduate at Technical University of Kenya I perused Bachelor
of Business Information Technology, 23yrs, Father of One Son and married
to one wife Beatrice Wanjiku. I was born in Kenya I am entrepreneur who has
always believed that working an 8-4 job isn`t the best way to live life.
After observing so many individuals follow the usual path of making a living.
I realized they were missing out on what life truly has to offer.
So, I decided to change that and create wealth through something I had passion for,
and that’s when I discovered fashion and Blog business which I saw as a big gap in the market
